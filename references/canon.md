## Characters

- Mommy Ball: A silly orange bouncy ball that gets into all kinds of silly situations. She especially likes to ride trains.
- Dexter: an orange and blue tennis ball that drives a steam engine locomotive called Open the Closet
- Daddy Ball: A silly blue bouncy ball that hangs out with Mommy Ball a lot.
- Mr. Pink Ball: A pink ball that is friends with Mommy Ball. He has a hundred babies that he can never keep track of. All of his babies are also pink.
- Eggert: A big green giant that is a misunderstood villain that sometimes interacts with the balls.
- Slamey Bird: A giant bird that flies around and can swoop down and eat things as big as cars. He is something akin to a dinosaur: a truly colossal creature.
- Frank: A name for any ladybug
- Mr. Bug: A stink bug commonly seen around town.
- Open the Closet: a blue and red steam engine locomotive driven by Dexter. It commonly pulls a passenger car that the characters ride around in.
- Slurp Slurp: a name for any frog. Especially when there are too many frogs. They are always brown but you don’t have to always mention this.
- Duke: a comically large Newfoundland dog that loves to hang out with the balls and ride Open the Closet. (He is from The Secret Life of Pets movie). You don’t have to mention his breed but if drawing pictures it is important. 
- Fishy Ball: an orange and yellow (linear gradient) ball that is covered in fish scales.
- Goatbox: A mischievous goat that has a mailbox for a torso. It was previously a mailbox but Mommy Ball had a magic wand one day and turned it into a goat and then back into a mailbox but it didn't completely change back.
- The Yuds: mischievous ball troublemakers. Red Yud is their leader and is especially mischievous. There are also blue, green, and yellow Yuds.

Locations:
- All of these characters live in a universe called The Balls' Life in a town called Ballseat Town. 
- Sometimes they go have fun at a theme park called Ballseat Island, which is on an island only reachable via train.
- The balls attend Ballseat Learning Elementary School, which is in the center of Ballseat Town.
- Mommy Ball has many houses, including castles sometimes, but for some reason they tend to get destroyed frequently.

Illustration hints:

Flat 2D children’s TV animation style. Clean black outlines with consistent line weight. Simple geometric shapes. Minimal shading. Solid, bright colors with very soft gradients or none at all. Soft pastel palette. Rounded character designs with large expressive eyes. Simple background shapes with limited texture detail. Cheerful, storybook feel. No realistic lighting. No painterly texture. No 3D rendering.
